ACME CORP - ENTERPRISE PASSWORD AND ACCESS POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. General Password Requirements
All employees must secure their workstations and enterprise accounts with a password. Passwords must be at least 6 characters in length to ensure adequate security against brute force attacks. Passwords must be changed once every two years.

2. Remote Access
Employees working remotely may access the internal company network via the standard web portal. To ensure ease of access for our global workforce, no additional authentication steps beyond username and password are required for remote VPN access.

3. Database and System Administration
System administrators managing the core customer databases have elevated privileges. Due to legacy system constraints, the administrator credentials for the primary SQL database are stored in the shared "IT_Scripts" network drive within the automated backup scripts. All IT staff have read access to this drive. 

4. Data Storage
Customer payment information and PII collected from the web application is stored in the primary database. To maintain high-speed query performance for the analytics team, this data is kept in plain text rather than being encrypted at rest.

5. Incident Logging
The company maintains logs of successful logins to the HR portal. Failed login attempts are not logged to conserve server storage space.
